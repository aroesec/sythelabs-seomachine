## 7. Hook Development

### Introduction Angle
Open with the "department of no" myth. Security teams have a reputation for blocking projects, slowing releases, and adding friction. But the data tells a different story. Companies that invest in security early grow faster, close bigger deals, and avoid the catastrophic costs that shut down 60% of small businesses after a breach.

### Value Proposition
Readers get data-backed proof that security is an investment with measurable returns, not a tax on productivity. Specific dollar figures, real scenarios, and a clear framework for thinking about security as a business enabler.

### Contrarian Element
Most security content uses fear. This article uses math. The argument isn't "you'll get hacked if you don't invest." The argument is "you'll grow faster, close more deals, and spend less money if you do invest." Flip the narrative from threat to opportunity.

### Sythe Labs Practitioner Perspective
Unlike competitor articles written by software vendors and analysts, this comes from a team that runs penetration tests, compliance audits, and vCISO engagements for startups and SMBs every week. Real-world observations, not theoretical frameworks.
